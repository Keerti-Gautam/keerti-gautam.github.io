Who am I ?

I'm Keerti. I was born and raised in New Delhi, India but my family hails from a small town located near Mathura. Engineering happened to me by chance, but my life in that college from first year onward, by choice. I am an engineer by formal records, but there is more to me than just that. I aspire to use my skills to assist, support and empower people socially, economically and technically. I am the recipient of James O. Gray Scholarship 2017, IEEE Computer Society Richard E Merwin Scholarship 2017 and Grace Hopper Conference India Scholar 2016.

Inclined towards encouraging women to follow their interests, I am committed to mentoring, helping and assisting women into STEM. I am a diversity freak and like to know different opinions coming from people of diverse backgrounds. And diversity isn't only about inluding women, ya know.

I am a self motivated person and I love working at the intersection of technology and business. I'm passionate about driving decisions based on data, developing websites and currently, software development. I am intrigued by wonderful projects and organisations (Processing, Internet Archive, Alaveteli, Physical Web etc), Economics, Strategy and Planning, Genetics, Astronomy, Literature, Jane Austen and sunsets.

“There is a stubbornness about me that never can bear to be frightened at the will of others. My courage always rises at every attempt to intimidate me.”

~ Elizabeth Bennet from Pride and Prejudice, Jane Austen

I am inspired by the world around me and so I love capturing sunsets, trees, birds, views, people and moments. I am usually endeavoring to go out of my comfort zone every now and then, striving to work really hard for things that are worth it, trying to stay close to people who bring the best in me, to question status quo and aiming to bring rest to the chaos that's my life.

I love listening to music. I very recently got addicted to The Lumineers and their album Cleopatra. My other favourites are OneRepublic, Linkin Park, James Blunt, Coldplay, Imagine Dragons, Sons of the East, John Mayer, Taylor Swift and Ed Sheeran. I have a playlist of soundtracks as well.

I am a blogger at Outlying Confluence. I am a conversationalist and love nice discussions over a cup of tea.
I paint, cook, dance, sing and play FreeCell in my spare time.
