Core Organising Team Member - Taarangana'17 June '16 - May '17

Women in Engineering (WIE) Coordinator - IEEE Delhi Section June '15 - May '17

Being the Women in Engineering Coordinator of IEEE Delhi SAC for more than two years, my work included coordinating WIE events with SAC's as well as schedule, manage and administrate standalone WIE events, which hadn't ever been proposed before.

Envisioned, conceptualised, structured and organised workshop series on OSS, Web Development etc for the Section and especially to target more women engineers to learn. These workshops had more than 200 student attendees with a 95% retention rate on the second day.

Organised various congresses, workshops, events, with primary focus on involving and encouraging more young women engineers into CS, Robotics and AI. Also took various sessions of IEEE WIE and why women should lean in to follow their interests and passion.

Worked as an Assistant Editor for the biannual letter of IEEE Delhi Section SAC, 'Eloquence' for both its print issues.

Assisted 6 student branches (MAIT, PEC etc) in the formation of WIE Affinity Groups. Re-activated more than 7 WIE Student Branch Affinity Groups to participate in WIE events across the section.
